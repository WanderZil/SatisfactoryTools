About StarRupture Items

The starrupture items catalog covers everything you will handle in a factory run: raw resources, refined materials, intermediate components, tools, consumables, and late-game products. Understanding how starrupture items flow through recipes is the key to scaling production without bottlenecks.

In StarRupture, most starrupture items are part of a chain: basic inputs are mined or harvested, processed into stable intermediates, then assembled into higher-tier goods. Those goods are continuously consumed by manufacturing, upgrades, and progression, so planning the per-minute balance (production vs consumption) matters.

Use this list of starrupture items to explore what each item is used for, which recipes unlock it, and how it affects your production network. As you unlock more recipes and buildings, starrupture items become more specialized—supporting automation, energy systems, and advanced crafting paths.

StarRupture Recipes define how raw materials and components are transformed into finished products through your manufacturing facilities. Each StarRupture Recipe specifies input ingredients, output products, production time, and the buildings required to execute it.
