Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that we collect and how we use it.

Log Data: Like many websites, we collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our site. This may include your computer's IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on those pages, and other statistics.

Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience on our site. Cookies are small files that a site transfers to your computer's hard drive through your web browser. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Local Storage: StarRupture Tools uses browser local storage to save your production calculations and preferences locally on your device. This data never leaves your computer and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Understand how users interact with our StarRupture production calculator
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Develop new features and functionality for the calculator
  • Debug technical issues and optimize performance

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics to help us understand how our site is being used. These services may collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, such as cookies or your IP address.

Analytics: We use analytics services to track website usage and performance. This helps us understand which features are most popular and how we can improve the StarRupture Tools calculator.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Local Data: All production calculations and saved data are stored locally in your browser. We do not have access to this information, and it is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share it.

5. Game Data

StarRupture Tools uses game data extracted from StarRupture, including items, buildings, recipes, and corporations. This data is used solely for the purpose of providing accurate production calculations and is not modified or redistributed in any way that violates the game's terms of service.

This is an unofficial fan-made tool and is not affiliated with Creepy Jar or StarRupture. All game-related trademarks and content belong to their respective owners.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or about StarRupture Tools, please contact us through our About page.
